Baiao Introduces TradingView to Upgrade the Public Market Analysis Experience

Baiao is introducing TradingView charting technology into its public market analysis website to provide a more intuitive charting environment, clearer technical analysis workflows, and a more credible review experience for visitors evaluating the product in a live website context.

Through this upgrade, users visiting Baiao's market analysis pages will be able to work with a chart experience that is better aligned with professional chart-reading habits, while the site continues to focus on public information display, technical interpretation, and educational analysis.

  1. Real-time chart observation

    TradingView provides a familiar visual environment for following market movement, comparing symbols, and reviewing price structure across multiple timeframes. For example, when readers want a public reference for a btcusd chart, they can use the linked TradingView symbol page to review market context and price behavior directly.

  2. More complete technical analysis tools

    The planned integration supports a more professional chart workflow, including clearer timeframe switching, drawing-based review, and more structured reading of support, resistance, trend continuation, and momentum changes.

  3. Better continuity inside the Baiao website

    Visitors will be able to review chart-focused analysis in the same public website environment without needing to switch between unrelated interfaces. This improves product readability during licensing review, business evaluation, and ordinary public use.

  4. Visible source attribution

    Baiao will preserve TradingView attribution wherever TradingView charting technology is presented. This keeps the charting technology source visible on the page and aligns the website with the required public branding context for the implementation.

Baiao's implementation remains focused on market analysis, financial data visualization, and educational chart interpretation. It is not a brokerage interface and does not provide order execution or custody services. The role of TradingView in this context is to strengthen the charting layer and improve the quality of public-facing analysis.

As the implementation moves forward, Baiao will continue to keep the TradingView branding context visible on relevant pages and maintain a clear separation between public preview areas, licensed chart mounts, and the website's own market data explanations.
